# 72IB Weekly Intelligence: Indian Private Markets

This repository serves as the official archive for institutional-grade deal data, macro analysis, and sector intelligence regarding the Indian technology and private markets. These reports are designed to provide founders, venture capitalists, and market participants with a clear view of capital velocity and institutional conviction.

---

## Overview

The reports hosted here map the flow of capital across the Indian ecosystem using a combination of disclosed transaction data and proprietary research. Each briefing delivers a granular breakdown of financing statistics to identify emerging trends before they become consensus.

### Research Scope
* **Financing Velocity:** Tracking weekly capital deployment and deal volume counts to measure market momentum.
* **Sector Intelligence:** Deep dives into high-conviction sectors including Ecommerce, Fintech, AI, and Clean Tech.
* **Capital Stratification:** Analysis of ticket size distribution from seed-stage rounds to late-stage growth equity.
* **Investor Participation:** Monitoring activity levels of major institutional backers and early-stage funds.
* **Macro Environment:** Contextualizing private market activity against global indices and domestic public tech performance.

## Project Structure

Reports are organized chronologically to maintain a historical record of market movements.
